SLC, UT - THURSDAY JANUARY 24, 2019 - FRONT PAGE
<h2>Area Man Studies Deli Menu</h2> <hr style="width:40%" id="horizontal"> <h3>By: Theodore Roosevelt</h3> <hr style="width:40%" id="horizontal">

It was a quarter past 4 today when a local area man decided
to come into the local deli and intently study the menu. He
could be seen glancing over the various options while
carefully considering the sandwiches it contained.

There were many choices to consider so this glancing took several
minutes. It was noted by several witnesses that eventually
the area man decided on a bacon turkey club... which is
notable different from his usual roasted chicken.

As far as we know, the man enjoyed the sandwich. Sources are
reporting they heard him say, "an easy 8/10". Area man is known
for being a rather tough critic so all sandwich makers were relieved
upon learning of these relatively high marks.
